Our Members

Cutting Edge #10909 is organized into five main sub-teams: Software & Programming, Outreach & Business, Build & Design, Electrical, and Strategy & Scouting. Each sub-team is led by a captain who coordinates activities and ensures effective communication within the team. This structure allows members to specialize in their areas of interest while contributing to the overall success of the team.

Software & Programming

Members of the Software & Programming team program our robots, website, software, and other digital assets.

Outreach & Business

Members of the Outreach & Business team manage our social media pressence, sponsors, donations, grants, and community outreach.

Build & Design

Members of the Build & Design team both construct and design our robots.

Electrical

Members of the Electrical team design and build the electrical systems for our robots, including wiring, sensors, motors, etc.
